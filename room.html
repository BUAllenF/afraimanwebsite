Room Occupancy Monitor

Introducing the Room Occupancy Monitor - a system that can sense how many occupants are in a room at a given time. When the user-set maximum occupancy is reached, a stop sign is deployed alongisde a buzzing sound to indicate that the room should not be entered. This ensures that the room maintains a safe occupancy level and complies with relevant safety regulations for the specific environment.

Materials

  • Arduino UNO
  • Ultrasonic Motion Sensors
  • LCD Screen
  • 3D Printed Battery Holder, Sensor Rack, Motor Case, Stop Sign
  • 28BYJ-48 Stepper Motor + ULN2003 Driver Test Module
